Tomatoe pasta sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large red pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vegetable stock OR a pinch of salt
  • Mushroom ketchup
  • Dried or fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Chop the onion into slices and fry in a large frying pan in the teaspoon of olive oil. Fry for approximately 5 minutes on a medium heat until the onions are a golden brown. Make sure you stir the onions regularly and try to have them on a heat that allows them to sweat, this makes them caramilise slightly and will make your sauce taste much sweeeter!
  2. Add the 2 tins of chopped tomatoes and turn the heat up.
  3. Chop the pepper into slices and add this to the sauce.
  4. Finally add all the other ingredients and bring the sauce to the boil. Then turn it down and leave it to simmer. The longer it is left on simmer, the tastier it will be. So aim to leave the sauce for as long as possible.
  5. Finally cook some pasta to eat with the sauce. I also like to eat this sauce with some rice and poached salmon.

Servings: 4
